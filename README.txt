lfs_trackactivity
=================

Django LFS ecommerce (http://www.getlfs.com/) activity tracking powered by http://www.adform.com.

Put into your settings.py module the right configuration, for example::

    ADFORM_PM = pmnumber
    ADFORM_ID = idnumer
